**Contract Agreement**  
**Between**  
**Luxury Resort Brands**  
And  
**Elite Home Furnishings, Modern Haven Decor, Elite Decorators, Signature Style Homeware, Trendy Furniture Co.**  

**Effective Date**: March 1, 2022

**Purpose**: The purpose of this contract is to outline the agreement between Luxury Resort Brands (hereinafter referred to as "The Client") and Elite Home Furnishings, Modern Haven Decor, Elite Decorators, Signature Style Homeware, Trendy Furniture Co. (hereinafter collectively referred to as "The Suppliers") for the provision of Furniture & Decor.

**Scope of Goods and Services**:  
The Suppliers will provide The Client with high-quality furniture and decor items, including but not limited to:
- Bespoke furniture pieces
- Room decor and accessories
- Custom upholstery services
- Window treatments
- Outdoor furniture and decor
- Lighting fixtures

**Terms and Conditions**:
1. **Contract Length**: This contract is valid for a period of two years from the start date, with an option for extension upon mutual agreement.
2. **Performance Metrics and SLAs (Service Level Agreements)**:
    - Delivery Time Frame: All orders must be delivered within 4-6 weeks from the order date.
    - Quality Assurance: All items must meet The Client's quality standards. Damaged or substandard goods must be replaced by The Suppliers at no additional cost.
3. **Payment Terms**: Payments will be made within 30 days of invoice receipt. Invoices must be submitted post-delivery and inspection of goods.
4. **Rebates and Incentives**:
    - The Client is eligible for a 5% rebate on annual spending exceeding $500,000 across all Suppliers.
    - A 2% early payment discount is available for payments made within 10 days of invoice receipt.
5. **Expectations for Delivery of Goods and Services**: 
    - All goods must be delivered to the specified location(s) by The Client.
    - The Suppliers are responsible for the insurance of goods until the delivery point.
6. **Key Contractual Requirements**:
    - The Suppliers must obtain prior approval for any subcontracted work.
    - Any changes to the agreed-upon items or services must be documented and approved by both parties.

**Guarantees and Warranties**:
The Suppliers guarantee that all furniture and decor items will be free from defects in material and workmanship for a period of one year from the delivery date. Any items found to be defective within this period will be replaced or repaired at no additional cost to The Client.

**Contract Extensions**:
This contract may be extended by mutual written agreement between The Client and The Suppliers, subject to the same terms and conditions or as renegotiated.
