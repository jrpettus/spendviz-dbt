### CONTRACT FOR MAINTENANCE & REPAIR SERVICES

#### BETWEEN LUXURY RESORT BRANDS AND TOTAL MAINTENANCE

This Contract is entered into on April 15, 2022, by and between Luxury Resort Brands (hereinafter referred to as the "Client") and Total Maintenance (hereinafter referred to as the "Supplier").

### 1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

The Supplier agrees to provide maintenance and repair services for the Client's properties, including but not limited to HVAC systems, plumbing, electrical systems, and general property upkeep. The Supplier is responsible for ensuring that all services are performed by qualified personnel and meet the Client's standards for quality and timeliness.

### 2. CONTRACT TERMS

This Contract shall commence on May 1, 2022, and shall continue for a period of twelve (12) months, ending on April 30, 2023, unless earlier terminated in accordance with the provisions of this Contract.

### 3. PAYMENT TERMS

The Client agrees to pay the Supplier on a monthly basis for services rendered, based on invoices submitted by the Supplier. Payment shall be made within thirty (30) days of receipt of a valid invoice. The rates for services shall be as agreed upon separately by both parties and annexed to this Contract.

### PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS AND REMEDIES

The Supplier is expected to respond to maintenance and repair requests from the Client within 48 hours of notification. Repeated failure to meet this expectation may result in a review of the Contract and potential penalties as mutually agreed upon by the parties.

### 5. GENERAL TERMS

This Contract contains the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements, whether written or oral. Any amendments to this Contract must be in writing and signed by both parties.
