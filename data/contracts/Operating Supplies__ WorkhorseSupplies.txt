**Contract Agreement Between Luxury Resort Brands and WorkhorseSupplies**

**Effective Date: March 1, 2022**

**1. Introduction**

This Contract Agreement (“Agreement”) is made between Luxury Resort Brands (“Buyer”) and WorkhorseSupplies (“Supplier”), collectively referred to as the “Parties.” This Agreement outlines the terms under which the Supplier will provide operating supplies to the Buyer.

**2. Scope of Goods and Services**

The Supplier shall provide various operating supplies, including but not limited to janitorial products, office supplies, and kitchen essentials necessary for the daily operations of Luxury Resort Brands. 

**3. Payment Terms**

The Buyer agrees to pay the Supplier within 30 days of receiving an invoice for the goods supplied. Payment amounts will be based on the price list provided by the Supplier at the time of the order.

**4. Delivery Terms**

The Supplier is responsible for delivering the goods to the Buyer's specified location within 14 days of an order being placed. Any delays in delivery must be communicated to the Buyer immediately.

**5. Termination**

This Agreement will automatically terminate on December 31, 2022, unless extended by mutual agreement of both Parties.

**6. Governing Law**

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the jurisdiction where Luxury Resort Brands is headquartered.
