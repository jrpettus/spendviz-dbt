**Contract for Marketing & Advertising Services**

**This Contract** is made as of May 15, 2022, by and between Luxury Resort Brands (hereafter referred to as "Client") and BrandBloom, StrategySavvy, SmartSparks, TargetedTactics, BrandBoosters (collectively referred to as "Suppliers").

**1. Services:**

The Suppliers agree to provide marketing and advertising services including but not limited to social media marketing, search engine optimization, content creation, and advertisement campaign management for Client. The specific services to be rendered will be discussed and agreed upon on a project-by-project basis.

**2. Payment Terms:**

Payment for services rendered by Suppliers will be due within 30 days of invoice receipt. Rates for services will be negotiated individually for each project based on the scope and complexity of the work required.

**3. Term and Termination:**

This Contract will commence on the date first written above and will continue until December 31, 2022, unless terminated earlier by either party with a 30-day written notice.

**4. General Provisions:**

This Contract contains the entire agreement between the parties regarding the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings, whether oral or written. This Contract may only be amended by a written document duly executed by both parties.

Given the strength of 1, this contract includes only basic elements such as services, payment terms, term and termination, and general provisions without delving into the strategic sourcing aspects like rebates, incentives, performance metrics, SLAs, delivery expectations, key requirements for certain types of goods and services, guarantees, warranties, or contract extensions.
