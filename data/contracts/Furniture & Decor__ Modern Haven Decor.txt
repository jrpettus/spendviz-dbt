**Contract Agreement**

**Between: Luxury Resort Brands**
**And: Modern Haven Decor, Elite Home Furnishings, Elite Decorators, Signature Style Homeware, Trendy Furniture Co.**

**Date of Agreement: January 15, 2022**

This Contract Agreement (hereafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into by and between Luxury Resort Brands (hereafter referred to as the "Client") and Modern Haven Decor, Elite Home Furnishings, Elite Decorators, Signature Style Homeware, Trendy Furniture Co. (collectively referred to as the "Suppliers"), collectively referred to as the "Parties."

**Scope of Goods and Services:**

The Suppliers agree to provide Luxury Resort Brands with a range of furniture and decor services, including but not limited to:
- Customized furnishing solutions
- Interior decor consulting and implementation
- Procurement of unique furniture pieces and decorative items
- Design and execution of thematic interior settings for various areas of the resorts

**Terms & Conditions:**

1. **Contract Term:** This Agreement is valid for a period of 2 years from the date of signing, with an option for renewal upon mutual agreement.

2. **Payment Terms:** Payment will be issued by the Client within 60 days of receipt and approval of the invoice submitted by the Suppliers. Invoices must include detailed descriptions of the goods and services provided.

3. **Delivery Expectations:** The Suppliers are expected to deliver goods and services in accordance with the schedule agreed upon. Failure to meet delivery deadlines may result in a reevaluation of the contract terms.

4. **Performance Metrics:** The satisfaction of Luxury Resort Brands in terms of quality, timeliness, and responsiveness of the Suppliers will be reviewed bi-annually. 

Given the strength of 2, the contract focuses on the foundational elements such as contract term, payment terms, delivery expectations, and a basic performance review system. It does not delve deeply into rebates, incentives, extensive terms and conditions, specific performance metrics and SLAs, detailed expectations for the delivery of goods and services, key contractual requirements for certain types of goods and services, guarantees and warranties, or sophisticated clauses for contract extensions.
