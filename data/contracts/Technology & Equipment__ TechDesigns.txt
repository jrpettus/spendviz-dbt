### Contract for Technology & Equipment Supply

This Contract is made and entered into on July 5, 2022, by and between **Luxury Resort Brands** (hereinafter referred to as "Buyer") and **TechGen, Quantum Equipment Solutions, EquipEvolve, & TechDesigns** (hereinafter collectively referred to as "Suppliers").

#### 1. Purpose

The purpose of this Contract is to outline the agreement between the Buyer and Suppliers for the procurement of technology and equipment necessary for the operation and enhancement of the luxury resort properties owned and managed by the Buyer.

#### 2. Supply of Goods and Services

Suppliers will provide the following goods and services to Buyer:

- **TechGen**: Latest in-room entertainment systems, including smart TVs and interactive guest service technologies.
- **Quantum Equipment Solutions**: High-end fitness equipment and wellness technology for spa and gym facilities.
- **EquipEvolve**: Advanced security systems, including surveillance cameras and access control technologies.
- **TechDesigns**: Custom lighting solutions and architectural technology enhancements.

#### 3. Payment Terms

Payment by Buyer to Suppliers will be made within 60 days upon the receipt of the invoice for the goods and services delivered.

#### 4. Delivery of Goods and Services

Suppliers are expected to deliver the goods and services within 90 days from the date of the order. Any delays must be communicated to Buyer at the earliest opportunity.

#### 5. Termination

Either party may terminate this contract with 30 days written notice to the other party.

This document represents the entire agreement between Buyer and Suppliers and supersedes all previous agreements whether oral or written.
