**CONTRACT AGREEMENT**

**BETWEEN LUXURY RESORT BRANDS**

**AND FLAMEMASTER TECH**

This contract is made between Luxury Resort Brands (hereinafter referred to as "the Buyer") and FlameMaster Tech (hereinafter referred to as "the Supplier"), collectively referred to as "the Parties," to procure Food & Beverage Equipment for Luxury Resort Brands' premises.

**1. CONTRACT TERMS**

This agreement shall begin on September 1, 2021, and will continue until August 31, 2022, unless terminated earlier as provided herein.

**2. PRODUCTS AND SERVICES**

The Supplier shall provide Food & Beverage Equipment, including but not limited to commercial ovens, grills, espresso machines, and refrigeration units. The Supplier is also responsible for the installation of said equipment and providing maintenance services as needed.

**3. REBATES**

For every cumulative purchase that exceeds $100,000 within the contract term, the Buyer shall receive a 5% rebate on the amount exceeding $100,000.

**4. INCENTIVES**

The Supplier will offer the Buyer one complimentary piece of equipment for every ten purchased within the contract period.

**5. TERMS AND CONDITIONS**

The Buyer agrees to purchase a minimum of $50,000 worth of equipment throughout the duration of the contract. Failure to meet this minimum requirement may result in renegotiation of contract terms.

**6. PERFORMANCE METRICS AND SLAs**

The Supplier guarantees a response time of 24 hours for maintenance calls and a resolution time of 72 hours for any equipment malfunctions. Failure to meet these service levels will result in a 5% discount on future purchases for every breach.

**7. PAYMENT TERMS**

Payment for the goods and services shall be made within 30 days from the invoice date. Late payments will incur a charge of 1.5% per month on the outstanding balance.

**8. DELIVERY OF GOODS AND SERVICES**

The Supplier shall ensure delivery of equipment within 4 weeks from the date of purchase order. Installation of equipment will be conducted no later than two weeks after delivery.

**9. WARRANTIES AND GUARANTEES**

The Supplier warranties all equipment to be free from defects in material and workmanship under normal use for a period of one year from the date of installation. During the warranty period, the Supplier will repair or replace defective parts at no cost to the Buyer.

**10. CONTRACT EXTENSIONS**

This contract may be extended for additional one-year periods, subject to mutual agreement of the terms by both the Buyer and the Supplier at least 60 days prior to the expiration of the current term.

This agreement represents the entire agreement between the Parties pertaining to its subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, negotiations, and discussions, whether oral or written, of the parties.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the Parties have executed this agreement as of the date first above written.
