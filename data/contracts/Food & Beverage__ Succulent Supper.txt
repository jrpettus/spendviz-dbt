**Contract for Food & Beverage Services Between Luxury Resort Brands and Succulent Supper, Refreshing Reprieve, & Gourmet Gulp**

---

**This Contract** is entered into on the 1st of June, 2021, by and between **Luxury Resort Brands** (hereinafter referred to as "Client") and **Succulent Supper**, **Refreshing Reprieve**, & **Gourmet Gulp** (hereinafter collectively referred to as "Supplier").

**WHEREAS**, the Client operates a series of luxury resort properties and is in need of quality food and beverage services to cater to their esteemed guests; and

**WHEREAS**, the Supplier specializes in providing top-tier food and beverage products, including but not limited to gourmet dishes (Succulent Supper), refreshing beverages (Refreshing Reprieve), and exclusive high-quality drinks (Gourmet Gulp); and

**WHEREAS**, both parties wish to enter into this contract for the provision of said services by Supplier to the Client;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, the parties hereto agree as follows:

1. **Scope of Services:**
   - **Succulent Supper** will provide gourmet meals and catering services.
   - **Refreshing Reprieve** will supply a variety of non-alcoholic and alcoholic beverages.
   - **Gourmet Gulp** will offer exclusive high-quality drinks and cocktail services.

2. **Payment Terms:**
   - The Client shall pay the Supplier for the goods and services provided on a monthly basis, upon receipt of an invoice from the Supplier. Payment shall be made within 30 days of receiving the invoice.

3. **Duration of Contract:**
   - This contract shall commence on the 1st of June, 2021, and shall continue in full force and effect until the 31st of May, 2022, unless terminated earlier by either party with a one-month prior written notice.

This Contract represents the entire agreement between the parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior oral or written agreements and understandings. This Contract may only be amended or modified by a written document executed by both parties.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto have executed this Contract as of the date first above written.
