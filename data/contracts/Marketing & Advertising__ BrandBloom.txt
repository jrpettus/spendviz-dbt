**CONTRACT AGREEMENT**  
**Between Luxury Resort Brands and BrandBloom, StrategySavvy, SmartSparks, TargetedTactics, and BrandBoosters**  
**Effective Date: April 15, 2023**

**PREAMBLE**  
This Contract Agreement (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is made effective as of the 15th day of April, 2023, by and between Luxury Resort Brands (hereinafter referred to as the "Client") and BrandBloom, StrategySavvy, SmartSparks, TargetedTactics, and BrandBoosters (collectively referred to as the "Suppliers"). The Client and the Suppliers may each be referred to as a "Party" or collectively as the "Parties".

**1. SCOPE OF SERVICES**  
The Suppliers shall provide comprehensive marketing and advertising services to the Client, including but not limited to digital marketing, brand management, content creation, social media marketing, search engine optimization, advertising campaigns, market research, and strategy development.

**2. CONTRACT TERMS**  
This Agreement shall commence on April 15, 2023, and shall continue until December 31, 2023, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement. The Agreement may be extended by mutual agreement in writing by both Parties.

**3. REBATES AND INCENTIVES**  
The Suppliers shall offer rebates to the Client for spend thresholds exceeding $500,000, at a rate of 1.5% of the total spend. Additional incentives, including bonus services and priority project status, shall be provided for continued partnership and increased annual spend.

**4. TERMS AND CONDITIONS**  
The Suppliers guarantee the delivery of high-quality services in accordance with the specifications detailed in Exhibit A. Any services deemed unsatisfactory by the Client shall be revised at no extra cost.

**5. PERFORMANCE METRICS AND SLAs**  
The Suppliers shall adhere to specific performance metrics and Service Level Agreements (SLAs) that include a minimum 95% on-time delivery rate for projects, a response time of 24 hours for all communications, and measurable improvements in the Client's marketing KPIs within the first six months of engagement.

**6. PAYMENT TERMS**  
Payment shall be made by the Client within 30 days from the receipt of each invoice issued by the Suppliers. A 2% late fee shall be applied to overdue payments exceeding this timeframe.

**7. DELIVERY OF GOODS AND SERVICES**  
The Suppliers are responsible for ensuring that all goods and services provided are of high quality, meet the Client's requirements, and are delivered in a timely and efficient manner in accordance with the agreed schedule.

**8. KEY CONTRACTUAL REQUIREMENTS**  
For certain types of goods and services, such as proprietary software or specialized advertising platforms, the Suppliers must ensure compliance with relevant laws, licensing agreements, and any third-party contractual obligations.

**9. GUARANTEES AND WARRANTIES**  
The Suppliers warrant that all services provided under this Agreement will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner. A 12-month warranty shall cover all services rendered, providing free corrective services for any work that fails to meet agreed standards.

**10. CONTRACT EXTENSIONS**  
The Client reserves the right to extend this Agreement for additional terms of one (1) year each, subject to the same terms and conditions, provided written notice is given at least 60 days prior to the expiration of the current term.

**IN WITNESS WHEREOF,** the Parties have executed this Agreement as of the Effective Date.
