Purchasing Contract (Further Revised)
This Purchasing Contract ("Contract") is made and entered into on this 1st day of July 2023, by and between Global Linens Inc., a company organized and existing under the laws of the State of New York, United States, with its principal place of business located at 123 Fabric Avenue, New York, NY 10001, hereinafter referred to as the "Supplier", and Luxury Hotel Group, a company organized and existing under the laws of the State of California, United States, with its principal place of business located at 456 Hospitality Lane, Los Angeles, CA 90015, hereinafter referred to as the "Buyer".

WHEREAS, the Supplier is engaged in the manufacturing and supply of high-quality linens;

WHEREAS, the Buyer is engaged in the operation of luxury hotels and resorts across the United States and desires to purchase linens for its properties;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained, the parties hereto agree as follows:

Products and Services: The Supplier agrees to sell, and the Buyer agrees to purchase, the linens described in Exhibit A attached hereto and made a part hereof, under the terms and conditions set forth in this Contract.

Order Process:

The Buyer shall issue purchase orders specifying the quantities of products to be delivered and the requested delivery dates.
Each purchase order issued by the Buyer and accepted by the Supplier shall be governed by the terms and conditions of this Contract.
Pricing, Discounts, and Rebates:

The prices for the products are as set forth in Exhibit A. These prices shall be fixed for the term of this Contract.
Volume Discounts:
A 5% discount on individual orders exceeding $10,000.
A 10% discount on individual orders exceeding $20,000.
Annual Spend Rebates:
A rebate of 2% on total annual spend exceeding $100,000.
A rebate of 5% on total annual spend exceeding $250,000.
Payment Terms:

Payment shall be due 30 days from the invoice date for each purchase order.
Payments shall be made in US dollars via bank transfer to an account specified by the Supplier.
Late payments shall incur a late fee of 1.5% per month on the outstanding balance.
Delivery and Acceptance:

The Supplier shall deliver the products to the locations specified by the Buyer in accordance with the delivery terms specified in each purchase order.
The Buyer shall inspect the products within 10 days of receipt. Acceptance of the products shall occur after the completion of the inspection unless the Buyer notifies the Supplier of any non-conforming products.
Term and Termination:

This Contract shall commence on the date hereof and shall continue in effect until one year from the date hereof unless earlier terminated in accordance with the provisions of this Contract.
Either party may terminate this Contract upon 30 days written notice to the other party.
Governing Law: This Contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

Dispute Resolution: Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Contract shall be resolved through arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.
